# HeartCare Analysis – Heart Disease Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

HeartCare Analysis is an interactive Power BI dashboard project designed to analyze heart disease trends, identify key risk factors, understand symptom patterns, and provide predictive healthcare insights using patient clinical data.

The project focuses on transforming raw healthcare data into meaningful visual insights that can support early risk identification and data-driven decision-making.

---

# Project Objectives

* Analyze heart disease distribution among patients
* Identify major clinical and lifestyle risk factors
* Understand symptom patterns associated with heart disease
* Perform patient risk stratification using analytical logic
* Create an interactive healthcare analytics dashboard for decision support

---

# Dashboard Pages

## Summary Dashboard

Provides a high-level overview of:

* Total patients
* Heart disease rate
* Average patient age
* Chest pain distribution
* Heart disease incidence across age groups

### Key Insights

* More than half of the patients were diagnosed with heart disease
* Asymptomatic patients formed the largest affected group
* Heart disease risk generally increased with age

---

## Risk Factors Dashboard

Analyzes major clinical indicators related to heart disease.

### Included Analysis

* Cholesterol vs Age relationship
* Resting Blood Pressure comparison
* Fasting Blood Sugar impact
* ST Slope analysis

### Key Insights

* High fasting blood sugar showed significantly higher heart disease risk
* Resting blood pressure was slightly higher among affected patients
* ST slope patterns showed strong association with heart disease

---

## Symptoms Dashboard

Focuses on symptom behavior and exercise-related indicators.

### Included Analysis

* Exercise-induced angina
* Maximum heart rate trends
* Chest pain type analysis

### Key Insights

* Exercise-induced angina was strongly associated with heart disease
* Maximum heart rate generally decreased with age
* Silent/asymptomatic heart disease cases were highly observed

---

## Predictive Insights Dashboard

Implements rule-based risk segmentation for patient prioritization.

### Risk Categories

* Low Risk
* Medium Risk
* High Risk

### Features

* Patient-level risk analysis
* Interactive filtering
* Risk distribution visualization

### Key Insights

* Patients were distributed across different risk levels
* Risk scoring helped identify patients requiring higher clinical attention
